# Precognito: Industrial User Manual

Welcome to the **Precognito** Predictive Maintenance System. This guide provides step-by-step instructions for technicians, safety officers, and managers to effectively use the platform.

---

## 🛠 For Maintenance Technicians

### 1. Accessing Work Orders
- Log in to the dashboard and navigate to the **Work Orders** page via the sidebar.
- Under the **"Tasks Assigned to You"** section, you will see all pending maintenance tasks generated by the AI system.

### 2. Field Check-In (QR Code)
- When you arrive at a physical machine, go to the **"QR Check-In"** tab on the Work Orders page.
- Click **"Start QR Scanner"** and point your mobile camera at the **Physical Asset Tag** on the machine.
- Alternatively, if the tag is damaged, use the **Manual ID Entry** field to type in the Asset ID (e.g., `MACHINE_1`).
- Successful check-in will be recorded in the immutable audit trail for ISO compliance.

### 3. Completing a Task
- Once the repair is finished, click the **"COMPLETE"** button on your assigned task.
- Provide a brief **Resolution Note** explaining the fix.
- Select any **Spare Parts** used from the dropdown menu to update the inventory automatically.
- Enter the **Actual Labor Hours** spent.
- Click **"Finalize & Close Ticket"** to submit the record.

---

## 🔥 For EHS (Environment, Health & Safety) Officers

### 1. Monitoring Thermal Safety
- Navigate to the **EHS Page** to see real-time temperature trends for all critical assets.
- Any asset exceeding **70°C for more than 5 minutes** will trigger a high-priority safety alert.

### 2. Subscribing to Mobile Alerts
- To receive urgent fire-risk alerts on your phone:
  1. Go to the **Settings** page in the dashboard.
  2. Install the **ntfy** app (Android/iOS).
  3. Subscribe to the topic listed in your settings (default: `precognito_alerts_demo`).
  4. Ensure your phone's notification priority is set to **"Urgent"** to bypass silent modes during emergencies.

---

## 📊 For Plant Managers & Executives

### 1. Viewing ROI and OEE
- Use the **Executive Dashboard** to monitor the **Overall Equipment Effectiveness (OEE)** of the entire facility.
- Review the **"Financial Impact"** section to see estimated cost savings from avoided emergency repairs.

### 2. JIT Inventory Management
- The **Inventory Page** will show **"Just-in-Time Logistics Alerts"** when an asset's Remaining Useful Life (RUL) is nearing the lead time of its required spare parts.
- Use the **"Spare Parts Inventory"** table to track stock levels and reorder points.

### 3. Generating Audit Reports
- Navigate to the **Reports** page.
- Choose a category (e.g., **Compliance Audit**) and select your date range.
- Click **"Generate"**, then download the resulting **Signed PDF**.
- These reports include formal company headers and signature blocks ready for quarterly verification audits.

---

## 🌗 UI Customization
- If working in high-glare or low-light factory conditions, use the **Theme Toggle** (Sun/Moon icon) in the top header to switch between **Dark Mode** and **High-Contrast Light Mode**.
